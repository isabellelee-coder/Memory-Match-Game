Create up to six matching pairs.

How setup works

Each card in the first row belongs with the card directly beneath it. Add text, a photo, or both to every item you would like to use.

Example 1 Photo of Grandma ↕ Grandma
Example 2 Photo of Dad ↕ My son, David
Example 3 Photo of Anna ↕ My granddaughter

The items will be shuffled when the player begins the game.
